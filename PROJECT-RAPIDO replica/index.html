Our Impact

Rapido is committed to making a positive impact on our cities and our planet. Here are some of the ways we're working to make a difference:

  • Reducing traffic: By providing a faster, more efficient alternative to cars, we're helping to reduce traffic congestion and emissions.
  • Creating jobs: We're proud to have created thousands of new jobs for drivers across India.
  • Empowering women: Through our Rapido Women's Safety Programme, we're working to create safe, secure employment opportunities for women.
  • Supporting the community: We're committed to giving back to the communities we serve, and we regularly partner with local organizations and charities to support their work.
